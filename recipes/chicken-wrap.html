Chicken Wrap

Description

This chicken wrap recipe is perfect for a prepared meal for work or school. The ingredients are suited for 2 wraps exactly and is easy to make. This recipe is great to add into your diet to make it e little better.

Ingredients

  • 200g chicken
  • 80g cream cheese
  • 50g light grated cheese
  • 100g tomato sauce
  • Tomato paste
  • Spinach
  • 2 protein wraps
  • Paprika powder
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Ultra bbq sauce from esn

Process

  1. Cut the chicken into cubes and marinate it with the spices and bbq sauce
  2. Mix the chicken with the cream cheese, tomato sauce, grated cheese, tomato paste and spinach
  3. Fill the two wraps with the mixed ingredients
  4. Fry both wraps with a little oil shortly and then put the wraps into the airfryer (180Â°C) for 15 minutes
